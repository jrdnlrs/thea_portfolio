Biography

The Story

"Fashion is the armor to survive the reality of everyday life."

Born in the Philippines and discovered at 16, Ellen Thea Lopez has spent the last decade shaping the visual language of modern luxury fashion.

She has opened and closed for Chanel, Saint Laurent, and Givenchy, appeared on the covers of Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and W Magazine.

  • Elite Model Management — Paris, London, Milan
  • DNA Models — New York
  • The Society Management — Los Angeles

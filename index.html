Biography

The Story

"Fashion is the armor to survive the reality of everyday life."

Born in Lyon and discovered at 16, Solène Moreau has spent the last decade shaping the visual language of modern luxury fashion. Her ability to transform — from the stark geometry of avant-garde runway to the intimate softness of beauty campaigns — has made her a constant presence in the industry's most demanding rooms.

She has opened and closed for Chanel, Saint Laurent, and Givenchy, appeared on the covers of Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and W Magazine, and fronted global campaigns for Dior and Acne Studios. Off the runway, she's known for her advocacy in sustainable fashion and her work with emerging photographers.

  • Elite Model Management — Paris, London, Milan
  • DNA Models — New York
  • The Society Management — Los Angeles
